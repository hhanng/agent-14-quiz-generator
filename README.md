# Quiz Generator from Notes

![Status](https://img.shields.io/badge/status-live-3a7030) ![Gemini](https://img.shields.io/badge/AI-Gemini%202.0%20Flash-4285F4) ![HTML](https://img.shields.io/badge/built%20with-HTML%2FJS-orange)

> Agent #14 of 100 | 100 AI Agents Challenge

Paste any lecture notes, textbook paragraphs, or study material and instantly get a full interactive quiz — multiple choice, true/false, or short answer. Track your score and review all answers at the end.

## Features
- Supports MCQ, True/False, Short Answer, and Mixed quizzes
- AI evaluates short answer responses in real time
- Score ring + full review at the end
- Download quiz as PDF for offline studying
- Quiz history saved locally
